Latest information and updates for the WinSock 2 SDK. From here you can also select the Knowledge Base tab and enter "Windows Sockets " or "WinSock" as a search phrase to find Knowledge Base Articles.

Stardust Technologies Inc
Always has the latest information about WinSock and related topics. This is the source for WinSock information.

Novell

Intel was heavily involved in the creation of WinSock 2 and provided many valuable resources to keep the project going. This is another good resource for the latest WinSock 2 specifications.
